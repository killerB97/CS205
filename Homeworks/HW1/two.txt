The manga has been adapted into an original video animation (OVA) produced by Production I.G in 1998, and an anime series produced by Toei Animation,
which began broadcasting in Japan in 1999 and has aired 754 episodes to date. Additionally, Toei has developed 
thirteen animated feature films, two OVAs, and five television specials. Several companies have
developed various types of merchandising such as a trading card game, and a large number of video games. 
The manga series was licensed for an English language release in North America and 
the United Kingdom by Viz Media and in Australia by Madman Entertainment. The anime series was licensed by 4Kids 
Entertainment for an English-language release in North America, 
before the license was dropped and subsequently acquired by Funimation in 2007.
