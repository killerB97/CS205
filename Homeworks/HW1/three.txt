Naruto was serialized in Weekly Shonen Jump magazine from the 43rd issue of 1999 to the 50th issue of 2014, with the 
chapters collected into seventy-two tankobon volumes by Shueisha. 
The manga was later adapted into a television anime, which was produced by 
Studio Pierrot and Aniplex. It premiered across Japan on the terrestrial TV Tokyo and other TX Network stations on October 3, 2002.
